About Dipped Delights

About Dipped Delights

Hi, I’m Lela, the creator behind Dipped Delights. I’m currently a college student at California State University, Long Beach, and I started this business as a way to turn my love for baking into something I could share with others.

What began as a creative hobby quickly grew into a passion for designing beautiful, handcrafted treats that make every occasion feel a little more special. From chocolate-covered strawberries and pretzels to cakesicles and custom dessert sets, each order is made with care and attention to detail.

I love working with customers to bring their ideas to life—whether it’s matching a color scheme, theme, or creating something completely unique for your event.

Dipped Delights is based in Long Beach, and I primarily offer local pickup. Delivery may be available for nearby areas depending on location—feel free to reach out to discuss options.

Thank you so much for your interest in my treats. Being able to turn my love for baking into something I can share with you is something I’m truly proud of.
